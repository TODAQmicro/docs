## Twins

A **Twin** is a digital wallet/merchant account in the TODAQ Micro system. It serves as:

- **Digital Wallet**: Holds balances of digital quantities (like USD)
- **Merchant Account**: Owns commodities and receives payments from their sales
- **Identity**: Has a unique hostname and key for identification

### Relationship to Commodities

Twins have a **one-to-many relationship** with commodities:
- One Twin can own multiple commodities
- Each commodity must have a `twin_id` linking it to its owning Twin
- When a commodity is purchased, payment goes to the owning Twin's balance

See [Commodities API](/reference/payment-api/v4/commodity) for creating products that belong to your Twin.
